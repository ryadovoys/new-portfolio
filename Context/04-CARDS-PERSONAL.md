# Personal & Inspiration Cards

These cards show personality and what inspires Sergey beyond work. They make the portfolio human and memorable.

---

## INSPIRATION & CULTURE

### Card: California Culture
**Text:** Inspired by skate, surf, snowboard culture. Old skate magazine aesthetics, 80s California vibe — it's part of who I am.
**Visual:** Collage of vintage skate/surf imagery or personal photos
**Priority:** Medium

### Card: Backcountry & Mountains

---

## NOTES ON PERSONAL CARDS

**Purpose:**
- Show Sergey is a real person with interests
- Create conversation starters for interviews
- Stand out from generic portfolios
- Connect with people who share similar interests

**Placement:**
- Mix with professional cards, don't separate
- Maybe 1 personal card per 4-5 professional cards
- Or group at the end as "What inspires me" section

**Tone:**
- Casual, genuine
- Not trying too hard
- Let the visuals speak
